Advisor Insight

This portfolio reflects a thoughtful, growth-oriented tilt with meaningful exposure to secular tailwinds in semiconductor and AI infrastructure — TSM, MU, and ASML collectively form a compelling "picks-and-shovels" bet on the ongoing chip supercycle. MSFT further reinforces the AI theme with a more defensive quality moat, while LLY provides excellent diversification into healthcare via one of the most compelling drug pipelines in a generation. The energy sleeve (XOM + BP) adds inflation sensitivity and solid dividend income, though BP's recent underperformance and the broader energy transition headwinds deserve watching. Notably, cash represents a substantial portion of the total account balance — while this provides optionality and a cushion against volatility, it is a meaningful drag on long-term returns in an inflationary environment, and deploying even half of it into a broad index fund or defensive dividend payer would materially improve capital efficiency. The single greatest risk remains concentration: over 44% of invested assets sit in technology, making the portfolio highly sensitive to a valuation reset in semis or a slowdown in AI capital expenditure — both realistic near-term scenarios that warrant a trim of TSM given elevated Taiwan geopolitical risk.
